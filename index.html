Research shows that felines can relieve stress and lower blood pressure, and studies have found that the hormone oxytocin is released when we're around pets, triggering feelings of happiness. With results like these, it's no wonder therapy cats are often taken to hospitals, nursing homes, schools and even prisons. This showroom is dedicated to breed cats that have specific behaviors in order to be good match for the type of therapy you need.
